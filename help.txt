--------------------
Commands for Tannin:
| s | store a password with a keyword
| r | retrieve a password by entering a keyword
| k | see a print out of all saved keywords (not yet implemented)
| c | copy a password to the clipboard (not yet implemented)
| p | paste a password from the clipboard (not yet implemented)
| w | wipe entire database (not yet implemented)
| h | view this help message
| q | quit Tannin

Keywords must be unique from each other, and they must not contain spaces, back-slashes, or pound symbols.

Tannin is designed to be used in tandem with Tea.lock, so passwords are only accepted if they are exactly 200 characters long.
